Wonderslate

Next gen learning platform

Overview

An educational deliverance, digital publishing, learning management and, a test preparation platform which allows the community of students, teachers, and publishers to learn, publish and share educational content the smart way, from anywhere, at any time.

The Problem

Educational content is the core of any education system, which connects the 3 main stakeholders i.e. Students, Teachers/Institutes, and Publishers. Them not talking to each other gave birth to the idea of Wonderslate where a technology-enabled deliverance system will connect all these verticals and improve the publishing, teaching, learning experience.

" Learning is not the product of teaching. Learning is the product of the activity of learners.

Product Vision

To build a revolutionary ecosystem where all the stakeholders in an educational system can come together and create a cohesive learning experience.

My Role

Over the past year , I am the part of the design team that led the implementation of the core wonderslate EDOS and also initiated a path breaking product via building and managing a team of developers , marketers and content creator so as to come up with a scalable solutions to overcome the humps in ed-tech space.

Got an immense opportunity to work closely with the company stakeholders/leaders in reimagining the product, to help the business scale and bring clarity and order to defined process.

User Research

After going around some schools and colleges and interviewing students and faculty member we came across some of the major pain points and and we made our business strategy around it.

My research encompasses

  • How to make the whole study process of students more convenient to have a better outcome?
  • How to provide teachers with a full Fledged LMS that caters their complete teaching process?

For Teachers.

  • After spending quality time with the teachers and other faculty members across the schools and colleges we noticed that all the LMS in the current market were relevant for the western education system and not the Indian education system which still relies on predefined curriculum and marking system
