BusBud

Your Own Transit Companion

Overview

In low and middle income countries, buses are likely to remain the primary mode of mass transit for the foreseeable future (Tiwari 1994). In most transportation scenarios, reliance on buses has a positive impact on maximum number of commuters around the country.

The Problem

5 days a week I travel by bus to get to work. I realize that the bus that I take to work tend to come on different timing every single day. Traffic may be the vital cause of this issue but this puts me in a situation where I have to think whether to wait for the bus or not. Quite the dilemma right?.

For commuters

To reach a place at a particular time, you expect to see particular set of buses over a window of time. But the timings of the buses are never the same. Lot of time is wasted because the buses observe no time schedule , some time the buses don't tend to turn up for more than 40 minutes and some other times the three of the same buses turn up together.

For Bus conductors

The passengers limit is not predictable and sometime uncontrollable . Due to this issuing the ticket to people become a mammoth task. They need to ask the people where they wanna go, then issue the ticket from their billing machine and give the change back. But their comes a time when they don't have the change to give and thus leading to brawl. Moreover they have to carry fat stack of cash all day around which they have to keep save no matter what.

" The job that the bus conductors do is supposedly a challenging as the nature of the work is always mobile in the bus and within the bus. Getting in and getting down from the bus frequently at every stop.

Product Vision

  • Supply a reliable and a simple app which tell you exact location of the buses, so people are never late.
  • Make a simple and a convenient way for the easy transaction of money between the commuters and conductors.

User Research

Since I had been travelling by bus to work for most of the time, I could find some commuters facing same kind of issues.

My research encompasses

  • Understanding the day to day issues of the commuters.
  • Roles and responsibilities of the conductors and drivers..

Survey Questions.

For commuters

Q1.Recall a time when a bus got delayed for a long time. What did you think or feel?

Q2. When a bus is delayed, what information would you want to know?

Q3.What is it that you feel when the bus conductor doesn't provide you with the change?

For Bus conductors

Q1.How difficult is it to manage people in a crowded bus?

Q2.Is it scary to carry a stack of cash all round the city for a whole day?

Q3. What is your opinion on the current practice of issuing tickets?
